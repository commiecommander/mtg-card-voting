How It Works

Vote on the power level of Commander cards based on your experience. Your input helps the community understand which cards are truly impactful in the format. There is no wrong vote that you can cast here. This is how you have experienced any given card in your time playing Commander.

Power Level 1

Would expect this card in Core.

Power Level 2

Would expect this card in an Upgraded deck but would accept seeing it in a Core deck.

Power Level 3

Would expect this card in any Optimized deck and would avoid it in Upgraded if another option existed.

Power Level 4

This card has no business outside of arranged High Powered Games or cEDH.
