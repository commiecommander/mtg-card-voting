MTG Card Voting Tool for Commander

Welcome to the community-driven power bracket voting website for Commander (EDH). This tool empowers the community to interpret and vote on the power levels of Magic: The Gathering cards in Commander, as defined by Wizards of the Coast.

Power Brackets for Commander

Bracket 1

Casual Commander: Relaxed game pieces focused on fun interactions with basic investment of game mechanics. Perfect for low-power, social games.

Bracket 2

Average Commander: Cards that may resemble Bracket 1 with additional upsides or Bracket 3 and 4 with drawbacks or requirements. Balanced for mid-power games.

Bracket 3

High-Power Commander: Conditionally powerful cards that can deliver strong outcomes but are still reasonably answerable by a table. Ideal for optimized decks.

Bracket 4

Competitive Commander: High-power cards that make decks far more consistent. These cards are often high-salt and suggested for competitive play. Not to be confused with cEDH, which is an entirely different level of power.
