MTG Card Voting Tool for Commander

Welcome to the community-driven power bracket voting website for Commander (EDH). This tool empowers the community to interpret and vote on the power levels of Magic: The Gathering cards in Commander, as defined by Wizards of the Coast.

For a detailed explanation of the new Commander Brackets system, visit this link.

Power Brackets for Commander

Bracket 1: Exhibition

Casual Commander: Ultra-casual, thematic decks with no focus on winning. No Game Changers, infinite combos, or mass land denial.

Bracket 2: Core

Average Commander: Similar to average preconstructed decks. No Game Changers, infinite combos, or mass land denial. Tutors and extra-turn cards are limited.

Bracket 3: Upgraded

High-Power Commander: Stronger than preconstructed decks, with up to three Game Changers allowed. No early-game infinite combos or mass land denial.

Bracket 4: Optimized

High-Power Optimization: High-powered decks with no restrictions (other than the banned list). Explosive starts, strong tutors, and combos are common.

Bracket 5: cEDH

Competitive Commander: Competitive Commander with a focus on tournament play and metagame. No restrictions (other than the banned list).
