What Are Game Changers?

Listed below are what Wizards of the Coast and the Commander Format Panel have decreed to be considered cards that are game changers. These cards have a significant impact on gameplay and are often pivotal in shaping the outcome of a match.

You can learn more about game changers here.

However, this page goes a step further. Through the power of the Scryfall Tagger Initiative, you can click on each Game Changer to view a page of cards that share one or more related tags. This allows you to explore whether it's the card itself or its effect that makes it truly game-changing.

As a community member, player, or someone who encounters these cards regularly, this tool empowers you to weigh in on what makes a card impactful. Is it the card, or is it the effect? The choice is yours.
