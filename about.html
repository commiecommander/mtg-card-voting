About This Page

Our Mission

The Commander Collective was born out of the fallout from the September 2024 bannings and the growing influence of Wizards of the Coast (WotC) over the Commander format. We believe in player power and the importance of a community-driven approach to the game we all love.

Why We Exist

The current system, where card evaluations and game-changing decisions are solely in the hands of WotC and the Commander Format Panel (CFP), leaves little room for the voices of the players. We disagree with this top-down approach and believe that the community should have a say in how our format evolves.

What We Stand For

Every content creator and player has their own experiences and perspectives on what makes a card powerful or format-warping. While it may seem impossible to create a unified rating system, we believe that together, as a community, we can. Our Vision

This site is a tool for the community to come together and share their insights. Whether it's voting on card power levels, discussing game-changers, or analyzing decks, we aim to create an unofficial check and balance to the decisions made by the Commander Format Panel. This is your platform to make your voice heard.

Join Us

If you believe in a community-driven future for Commander, register to vote, share your experiences, and help us build a more inclusive and player-focused format. Together, we can ensure that Commander remains a format by the players, for the players.
